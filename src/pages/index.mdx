# About Platform

**Text** is an online customer service software with live support, help desk, and web analytics capabilities. It's used by more than <Placeholder id="NUMBER_OF_CUSTOMERS" /> companies in over 150 countries. **Platform** is an ecosystem for developers that grew mainly around two Text products, <a href="https://www.livechat.com/" target="_blank">LiveChat</a> and <a href="https://www.helpdesk.com/" target="_blank">HelpDesk</a>.

From the technical perspective, the core of the Platform is the **open messaging protocol**. The Messaging APIs let you integrate any communication channels directly into LiveChat product. From the business point of view, we're a team dedicated to build vivid [LiveChat Marketplace](https://www.livechat.com/marketplace/) and HelpDesk Marketplace full of apps and services created by <a href="https://developers.livechat.com/" target="_blank">our community of developers</a>.

We've just scratched the surface. There is much more for you to discover! Go ahead and see the full Platform Overview, or dive deep into Platform Toolkits.
